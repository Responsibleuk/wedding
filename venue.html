Carousel

19-23 Charlotte St, London, W1T 1RW

Since opening in Marylebone five years ago, Carousel’s award-winning creative hub has been home to an ever-changing line-up of international guest chef collaborations, workshops, exhibitions and all round awesome experiences, morning, noon and night.

Big name chefs from all over the world, pasta rolling, miso making, food photography… You name it. No two days here are ever the same. They offer events of all different shapes and sizes, offering up top-notch food and drink, friendly service and creative in-house productions. On the 18th of November they will be hosting our wedding.

Food & Drink

Inspired by the crowd-pleasing seasonal small plates from Carousel’s critically-acclaimed wine bar menus, the food will be seasonal and delicious, but please do let us know if you have any food allergies.

All produce is sourced from ethically-minded UK farmers, foragers and fishermen who share Carousel’s values, and the wine list is also focussed on small, independent producers.
